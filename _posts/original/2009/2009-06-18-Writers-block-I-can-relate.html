I Can Relate

What fictional character do you most identify with?

At the moment, I've been reading Watership Down (After many years of dismissing at as unreadable.), and now I've finally got to the end of it, I'm feeling like Hazel being told: "All the world will be your enemy, Prince with a Thousand Enemies. And whenever they catch you, they will kill you. But first, they must catch you, digger, listener, runner. Prince with a swift warning. Be cunning and your people will never be destroyed."
